# Stepping Back from Reflection

You realized your mistake and stepped back. *I was trying to force reflection,* you thought. *But reflection can't be forced. It needs to happen naturally, in its own way, in its own time. I need to let it be.*

You moved away, giving people space, letting them reflect however they needed to, whenever they were ready. And as you did, you saw something beautiful happen.

The celebration began to recover. The joy that had been fading began to return. People began to reflect naturally, in their own ways, in their own time. Some talked, some were quiet, some moved, some sat still. But they were all reflecting, understanding, preparing, in the way that worked for them.

*This is better,* you realized, watching as the celebration became more joyful, as people reflected naturally, as understanding grew. *By stepping back, by letting reflection happen naturally, I'm respecting people's process, their needs, their understanding.*
