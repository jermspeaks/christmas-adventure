# Broken Trust

You decided to take control, to tell Kvothe what to do, to make all the decisions yourself. You thought you knew best, that you should be in charge. You took control, pushing Kvothe aside, making them follow your lead.

*I know what's best,* you thought. *I should be in charge.*

But taking control only made things worse. Kvothe felt dismissed, unheard, controlled. They began to resist, to push back, to lose trust in you. The connection between you broke, and the magic began to fade.

*Maybe I shouldn't have taken control,* you thought, but it was too late.

The Keepsake Keeper's light dimmed, and you could feel its disappointment. "You took control," it said, its voice sad. "You dismissed Kvothe, made them follow your lead, but partnership requires trust, requires respect, requires working together. You took control, and Kvothe lost trust, the connection broke, the magic faded."

*I took control,* you realized. *I broke the trust, and I lost everything.*
