# Sharing What You've Learned

You turned to the others, sharing what you'd learned about Kvothe, about their past, about their transformation. "Kvothe was trying to protect people," you said. "They saw the magic fade, saw the disappointment, saw the pain, and they thought they could protect people by locking the magic away. But in doing so, they protected people from joy, not just from disappointment."

You shared the understanding you'd gained, helping others understand Kvothe, understand their transformation, understand what they were becoming. And as you did, you saw understanding spreading, connection growing, support strengthening.

*This is working,* you thought, watching as understanding spread, as people began to support Kvothe, as connection grew. *By sharing what I've learned, I'm helping others understand, helping them support Kvothe, helping them connect.*
