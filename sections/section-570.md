# Kvothe's Solitude

You moved away from the clearing, finding a spot beneath a tree where you could still see Kvothe but wouldn't intrude. The Keepsake Keeper pulsed softly in your hands, and you could feel its concern.

"They need time," you said, more to yourself than anyone. "They'll be okay."
