# Abandoned

You decided to give up, to let the magic sort itself out, to stop trying to help. You were tired, frustrated, and you just wanted to stop. You walked away, leaving the magic to its own devices.

*I can't do this,* you thought. *I'm giving up. Let the magic figure it out on its own.*

But the magic couldn't figure it out on its own. It needed guidance, needed help, needed someone to show it the way. Without that guidance, it became more unstable, more chaotic, more dangerous.

*Maybe it will work out,* you thought, trying to convince yourself. *Maybe the magic will figure it out.*

But it didn't. The magic became permanently unstable, chaotic, dangerous. It would never find balance, never stabilize, never work properly. And you had given up, had walked away, had abandoned it.

*I gave up,* you realized. *I walked away, and I abandoned the magic. I abandoned everyone who needed help.*

The Keepsake Keeper's light dimmed, and you could feel its disappointment. "You gave up," it said, its voice sad. "You walked away, abandoned the magic, abandoned everyone who needed help. But magic needs guidance, needs help, needs someone to show it the way. Without that guidance, it became permanently unstable, chaotic, dangerous. It will never find balance, never stabilize, never work properly. And you gave up, walked away, abandoned it."

*I gave up,* you realized. *I abandoned everything, and I lost everything.*
