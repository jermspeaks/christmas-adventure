# Incomplete Transformation

You restored the magic, but Kael didn't fully transform. They helped you open the vault, they worked with you, but their transformation was incomplete. They remained partially the guardian of shadows, partially the person they were meant to be.

*The magic is restored,* you thought, relief mixed with concern. *But Kael isn't fully transformed.*

Kael looked at you, their expression a mixture of gratitude and uncertainty. "I helped," they said. "I worked with you. But I'm not fully myself yet. I'm still... caught between who I was and who I'm meant to be."

*They're not fully transformed,* you realized. *The magic is restored, but Kael's transformation is incomplete.*

The Memory Keeper's light pulsed softly. "The magic is restored," it said. "But Kael's transformation is incomplete. They helped, they worked with you, but they're not fully themselves yet. The world has magic again, but Kael is still struggling."

*I should have helped them more,* you thought. *I should have ensured their transformation was complete.*
