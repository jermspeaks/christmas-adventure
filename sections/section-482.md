# Local Restoration

You restored the magic to Starlight Hollow, but not to the wider world. The magic flowed back to the village, bringing joy and wonder to the people there, but it didn't spread beyond. The rest of the world remained without magic.

*Starlight Hollow has magic again,* you thought, satisfaction mixed with concern. *But the rest of the world doesn't.*
