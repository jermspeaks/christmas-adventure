# Dismissed Concerns

You dismissed the guardian's concerns as unimportant, thinking they were just excuses, that you didn't need to understand them. You pushed forward, ignoring their pain, their reasons, their perspective.

*Their concerns don't matter,* you thought. *I just need to open the vault.*

But the guardian's concerns were important. They had reasons for locking the magic away, and without understanding those reasons, you couldn't truly help. You tried to force the vault open, but it didn't work. The guardian resisted, and the magic remained locked.

*This isn't working,* you realized, but it was too late.

The guardian became more resistant, more determined to keep the magic locked away. You couldn't convince them, couldn't help. The magic continued to fade, and Christmas Eve arrived. The magic disappeared completely, and Christmas faded forever.

*I should have listened,* you thought. *I should have understood their concerns.*
