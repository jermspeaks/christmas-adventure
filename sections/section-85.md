# Verifying the Handwriting

You found Officer Martinez and showed her the torn note fragment. "I think this matches Marcus's handwriting," you said. "But I want to be sure. Can you have it analyzed?"

Officer Martinez examined the note carefully. "We can do that," she said. "We have handwriting experts who can compare it to samples. But we'll need handwriting samples from Marcus to compare it against."
