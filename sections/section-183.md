# Balancing Celebration and Challenges

You decided to work with the community to address the challenges while still celebrating. You approached the people who were struggling, offering help, support, guidance. You worked with the places that were unbalanced, helping them find balance, stability. You supported the fragile connections, helping them grow stronger, more certain.

*This is what it should be,* you thought. *A community that celebrates, but also addresses challenges. A community that finds joy, but also helps, supports, addresses problems. A community that finds balance.*
