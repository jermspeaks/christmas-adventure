# Overwhelmed Kvothe

You decided to push Kvothe to take on more than they were ready for, to give them too much responsibility too quickly. You thought they could handle it, that they were ready. You pushed them, trying to make them do more, be more, take on more.

*They can handle it,* you thought. *They're ready for more.*
