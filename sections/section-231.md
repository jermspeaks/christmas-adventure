# Broken Connections

You decided to break connections with others to focus on yourself, to be independent, to not need anyone. You thought you could do it alone, that connections were holding you back. You broke the connections.

*I don't need anyone,* you thought. *I can do it alone.*

But breaking connections only made the magic fade from you. It required connection, sharing, togetherness. Without it, the magic left you, fading away completely.

*The magic is fading,* you thought. *It's leaving me.*

*I broke them,* you realized. *I lost the magic.*
