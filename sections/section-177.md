# Spreading Understanding

You continued teaching, and slowly, understanding began to spread. People were learning, one by one, how to connect, how to remember, how to share. They were learning that the magic wasn't about control—it was about connection, about memory, about emotion.

*It's working,* you thought. *People are learning. They're understanding. They're connecting.*

You watched as Elara helped others, as she shared what she'd learned, as she guided them, taught them, helped them understand. And you could see the understanding spreading, like ripples in a pond, one person teaching another, one connection leading to another.

*This is how it should be,* you thought. *Not one person controlling everything, but everyone learning, everyone sharing, everyone connecting. Not power, but connection. Not control, but understanding.*

Lumina trotted forward, their eyes bright. "The understanding is spreading," they said. "People are learning, they're connecting, they're sharing. They're understanding that the magic isn't about control—it's about connection, about memory, about emotion."

*I'm teaching them,* you thought. *I'm helping them understand, helping them learn, helping them connect. And they're learning. They're understanding. They're connecting.*

You continued teaching, and you could see the magic responding, flowing through the town, warm and steady, responding to memories, to emotions, to connections. The town was beginning to glow, to sparkle, to come alive with the magic of connection, of memory, of emotion.
